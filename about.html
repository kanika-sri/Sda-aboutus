Our Journey & purpose

Seventh-day Adventist Matric Higher Secondary School, Tenkasi, was founded with a vision to provide quality education with strong values. Over the years, we have grown into a trusted institution, shaping young minds through discipline, dedication, and holistic development. Our purpose is to empower students with knowledge, character, and confidence. We strive to create responsible individuals who are prepared to face the future and contribute positively to society.

Meet Our Leadership

Principal

Education is not just about learning lessons, but about shaping character and building confidence. At our school, we strive to create an environment where every student feels inspired to grow, explore, and achieve their best. We believe in nurturing discipline, curiosity, and values that stay for a lifetime. With the support of our dedicated teachers and parents, we guide our students towards a bright and successful future.

Treasurer

Our institution stands on a strong foundation of trust, responsibility, and commitment to quality education. We continuously work to provide the right resources and a supportive environment for every student to succeed. With careful planning and transparent management, we ensure the steady growth of the school. Our goal is to build not just an institution, but a place where education and values go hand in hand.
